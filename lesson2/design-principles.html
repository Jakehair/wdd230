Design Principles Document

Jacob T. Hair

Alignment

Mubasic

https://www.mubasic.com

This website is a great example of good alignment and is very consistent all the way down the page. The alignment helps focus the user on what needs to be seen.

Visual Hierarchy

SuperList

https://www.superlist.com

This site has a cool slider feature at the top which allows you to see the 2 main purposes of this app. The colors pop out and the most important things are shown at the top of the page in big bold text to get the main point across fast.

White Space and Clean Design

Wealthsimple

https://www.wealthsimple.com/en-ca

This site makes good use of white space and it feels minimalistic and focused. The headings and content is all centered and has cool parallax and animations as you scroll down, but all of this never takes more room than needed, there is enough empty spacing between everything so it feels comfortable.
